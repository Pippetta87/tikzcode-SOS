Combination rule for statistical fluctuation of indipendent variables (Poisson-Gauss distribution) and ''conservative''
\begin{align}
&(\Delta Q)_{sta}=\pm\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\sum_k(\delta Q)^2_k}\\
&(\Delta Q)_{con}=\pm|\sum_k(\delta Q)_k|
\end{align}
